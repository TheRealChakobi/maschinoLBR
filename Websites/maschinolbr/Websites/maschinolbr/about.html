About Us

We are always here to sever you!


We as Maschino & Sons Lumber Co. are always here to serve your every need. As a small business in southern maine since 1970 we know what community stands for. Through the years we have seen our community grow and prosper. Since the time we started with just a small building and one saw. We have now expanded two multiple buildings including a two full sized kilns. A wood planer, bagger, shaver, and we have a medium capacity mill. Making everything from all dimensions of hemlock and pine. To specialty orders such as red oak and spruce. Everything at Maschino Lumber is family. We strive ourselves on being a small business that is apart of the community. When you come to Maschino Lumber you will feel right at home with our two wonderful office ladies. When it's time to either pick up or load up we are always here to lend a hand.
